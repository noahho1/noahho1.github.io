U16 Hockey

The U16 Hockey team managed to put things together yesterday as hosts for the U16 Final Four Championship. The Blues first defeated St. Andrew's College 6-3 in the semifinal, setting up another encounter with Crescent School. Both games with Crescent during the regular season ended up in ties. The team knew that they would have to play their best game of the year to win the CISAA Championship. The Blues got out to an early 3-0 lead but Crescent did not quit. In the end, the U16 team won the game 4-2 on an empty net goal, sealing the CISAA Championship. Congratulations to the team on a terrific year which saw them lose only one game.
